About Me

I’m a German-American software developer and I made this website to share my work.

I’ve worked as a freelance programmer in Edinburgh and Berlin, an educator in Washington D.C. and Texas, and a lead programmer for an exhibit design company in Houston. Now I'm a front-end developer in Regensburg at Dallmeier Electronic GmbH.

I’ve taken on leadership roles on software projects and worked closely with researchers, graphic designers, and animators on tight schedules.
