About Me

I’m a German-American software developer and I made this website to share my work.

I’ve worked as a freelance programmer in Edinburgh and Berlin, worked as an educator in Washington D.C. and Texas, and filled the role of the lead programmer for an exhibit design company in Houston. Now I'm a front-end developer in Regensburg at Dallmeier Electronic GmbH.

As a programmer, I’ve taken on leadership roles on software projects and worked closely with researchers, graphic designers, and animators on tight schedules.
